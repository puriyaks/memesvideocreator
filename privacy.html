Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SwipeVideo collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic account data from TikTok or Instagram (if you log in).
  • Swipe preferences (liked/skipped videos).
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type (for analytics and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our swipe-based video service.
  • To remember your preferences and saved videos.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Storage

All saved videos are stored locally or on secured servers. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use TikTok and Instagram APIs to retrieve video content. Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
